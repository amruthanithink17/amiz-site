AmizClothz — Free Shipping Policy

Enjoy Free Shipping on Orders Over $50

We’re thrilled to offer free, fast, and reliable delivery to our valued customers!

Shipping Coverage

Free standard shipping applies to all orders over $50 across major cities and towns. For remote areas, minimal delivery charges may apply depending on courier availability.

Delivery Time

Our standard delivery time is 3–5 business days. During peak seasons, please allow up to 7 days. Express delivery options will be added soon for faster service.

Returns & Exchanges

Returned items are shipped back free of charge when the order qualifies for free shipping. For non-qualifying orders, standard courier charges may apply.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you’ll receive an email with the tracking ID and courier details. You can also check your shipment status anytime through your AmizClothz account.
